Open-world survival skirmish

Kingly Kings

Lead a levelling warlord through a living forest frontier. Defeat roaming warbands, recruit the fallen into your army, and survive an escalating medieval nightmare.

Player 1 uses hold-left-click mouse movement.

Player 2 uses a connected gamepad. Arrow keys are also enabled as a local fallback.

Press Tab anytime to toggle the controls sheet.

Controls

Player 1

Hold Left Click: Move toward the cursor while held

Click away while fighting: Attempt to break contact and escape

Mouse Wheel / +/-: Zoom the camera in and out

Tab: Toggle this controls sheet

Player 2

Gamepad Left Stick: Move

Arrow Keys: Local fallback movement

Run away: Pull distance until the enemy loses contact

Combat

Touching an enemy starts melee exchanges.

Winning recruits the defeated warband into your army.

Total health comes from base health, level growth, and recruit count.

Enemy Species

Vampires, werewolves, orcs, ogres, witches, skeleton knights, goblin sappers, treants, boar riders, and cultists stalk the map.
