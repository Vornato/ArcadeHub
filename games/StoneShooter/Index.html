Trio Breaker

Arcade Puzzle Action

Controls: WASD / Arrows to Move • Space/Enter to Pick • R to Reset

How to Play

1. Goal: Clear all stones from your board.

2. Rule: You can only remove the BOTTOM stone of a column.

3. Triplets: You must pick exactly 3 stones of the same color in a row.

4. Chain: Once you pick a color, you are locked to it until you find 3.

5. Versus: Clearing a triplet sends a garbage row to your opponent!

6. Stuck? If you mess up the order, press R (P1) or U (P2) to reset.
