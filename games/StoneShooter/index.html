Trio Breaker

Arcade Puzzle Action

Controls: WASD / Arrows to Move • Space/Enter to Pick • R / U / Pad B to Reset Your Board

How to Play

1. Goal: Clear all stones from your board.

2. Rule: You can only remove the BOTTOM stone of a column.

3. Triplets: You must pick exactly 3 stones of the same color in a row.

4. Chain: Once you pick a color, you are locked to it until the triplet is finished.

5. Readability: Matching bottom stones stay bright while invalid columns dim out.

6. Garbage: Incoming junk is queued before it lands. Your next clear can cancel it.

7. Fair Versus: Every versus round starts from the same seeded board for all players, and the round winner gets 1 point.

8. Final Win: Reach 5 round wins to take the full match.

9. Difficulty Ramp: Sessions begin on smaller boards with fewer stones, then grow larger and denser as levels and rounds go up.

10. Reset: In versus, each player can reset only their own board. In solo, resets still cost you.
